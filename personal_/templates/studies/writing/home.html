I. Process of writing

This are steps to follow to write a paragraph, good writes go through serval steps to produce a piece of writing.

  1. Pre-writing
    • choose a topic
    • Gather ideas
    • Organise ideas
  2. Drafting
    • Writing the first version
  3. Revewing and revising
    • Review/revise the structure and the content
  4. Rewriting
    • Check the spelling and grammar (Proofread)
    • Make a final correction
3 and 4 can be repeated many times

II. Pre-writing

before you write a paragraph you should choose and narrow a topic. Steps:

  1. Choose a narrowing topic

    Topic : the main idea or general idea of the paragraph

  2. Brainstorming

    gathering ideas, write every idea that comes to your mind, three types of brainstorming:

    • Making a list
    • Freewriting
    • Mapping

  3. Edting

    After the brainstorming (Gathering ideas) it's the time of editing or filtering the ideas to keep the most interesting and relevent to the topic.

III. The structure of a paragraph

  • The topic sentec: The main idea of the paragraph, it's usually the first sentence of the paragraph
  • The supporting sentences: sentences that talk/explain our topic
  • The concluding sentence: the last paragraph the gives a final comment about the topic

IV. The development of a paragraph

After choosing a topic sentence, you develop your main idea by adding information to explain what you mean.
Ways to develop a paragraph:

  • Details: Specific points that tell more about a general statement
  • explanation: tell the reader what something means or how something works
  • Example: develop you paragraph by providing examples about the topic

Peer editing: Showing your work to a student and vice versa
