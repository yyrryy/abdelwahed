When the phone rang, the baby woke up.

Important!!

"When the phone rang" is an adverb clause of time (time clause), used to indicate time, it's a dependent clasue, the second part is the main clause and it's independent, in order too show the depndency, you need to put a comma (,) after the time clause with capiralization.

You can start with the main clause if you want to avoid all of this
The baby woke up when the phone rang.

Never use FUTURE in a time clasue.

We will have a big partly when you will graduate X

We will have a big partly when you graduate.
